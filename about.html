About Nivanza

Nivanza is a multidisciplinary interior design firm led by founder and chief creative officer. The Nivanza signature aesthetic is defined by beautifully balanced spaces that are relaxed but elevated.

Nivanza is a multi-disciplinary interior design firm based in Salt Lake City, Utah, known for creating beautifully balanced spaces that are both classic and current.

Our mission is to Make Life
Beautiful for ourselves and those around us.

Led by husband-and-wife team Syd (Chief Executive Officer) and Shea (Chief Creative Officer), Nivanza Inc. is involved in both the interior design and product sides of the home design business with the company’s portfolio of brands, Nivanza. Nivanza is first and foremost an interior design firm, growing into a multi-disciplinary design house and content hub since its inception in 2014.
